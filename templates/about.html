About the Project

Book recommendation systems have become an essential feature of online book marketplaces, digital libraries, and e-reading platforms. These systems analyze user interactions, including book ratings, purchase history, and reading behavior, to suggest books that align with individual preferences.

Our mission is to create a personalized reading experience that helps you discover your next favorite book.
